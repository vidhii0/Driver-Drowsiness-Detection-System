About Our Project

Background

Our project was born out of a passion for improving road safety and preventing accidents caused by driver drowsiness. According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA), drowsy driving is responsible for an estimated 100,000 crashes each year in the United States alone.

Features

  • Real-time detection of driver drowsiness using computer vision technology
  • Audio and visual alerts to notify drivers when signs of drowsiness are detected
  • Easy integration into existing vehicle systems
  • Customizable settings for sensitivity and alert preferences

Benefits

By using our project, drivers can significantly reduce the risk of accidents caused by drowsy driving, leading to safer roads and fewer fatalities. Studies have shown that drowsy driving is just as dangerous as drunk driving, and our project aims to address this issue head-on.

Importance

Drowsy driving is a major contributor to road accidents worldwide. It not only endangers the lives of drivers but also passengers, pedestrians, and other road users. Our project provides an effective solution to this problem by alerting drivers when they show signs of drowsiness, allowing them to take appropriate action and avoid accidents.

Future Plans

We're committed to continuously improving our project and exploring new ways to enhance road safety. In the future, we plan to incorporate machine learning algorithms to further improve the accuracy of drowsiness detection and develop mobile applications for easy access and integration. Additionally, we aim to collaborate with automotive manufacturers to integrate our technology directly into vehicles, making roads safer for everyone.
